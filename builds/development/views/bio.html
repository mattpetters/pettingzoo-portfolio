bio


Pettingzoo is a band from Atlanta, GA.


In March 2014, we released our self-titled debut EP.


Summer 2014, we recorded an album titled Please, Stop Playing, and released it on January 20th, 2015.


Pettingzoo is Matt Petters (guitar and vocals), Cole Hairston (drums and percussion), and Moon-Tae Kim (bass).


Find us on Spotify and iTunes.
